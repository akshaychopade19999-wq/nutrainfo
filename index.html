Nutrition Calculators

Six precision tools to help you understand your body's needs — from daily calories to micronutrient targets.

Body Mass Index

BMI is a screening tool that uses your height and weight to estimate body fat. While not perfect for athletes or pregnant individuals, it's a useful starting point.

Total Daily Energy Expenditure

TDEE estimates your total daily calorie burn using the Mifflin-St Jeor equation, considered the most accurate by the Academy of Nutrition and Dietetics.

Estimate your optimal daily water intake based on body weight, activity level, and climate. Adjust based on thirst and urine color.

About NutraInfo

Why Evidence
Matters

The nutrition space is flooded with misinformation — detox teas, magic supplements, and fear-mongering about single ingredients. NutraInfo cuts through the noise by linking every recommendation to actual peer-reviewed research.

Our calculators use clinically validated equations (Mifflin-St Jeor, Devine, etc.), and our research summaries are written in plain language so you can make informed decisions without a PhD.
